FAQ

Key answers before project start: timeline, estimate, payments, materials, and warranty.

How quickly do you provide an estimate?

Usually the same day as the site visit or within 24 hours. For larger projects, we provide a detailed estimate with phases and timelines.

Do you handle turnkey multi-trade projects?

Yes. We coordinate multiple trades in one schedule: construction, renovations, plumbing, carpentry, and exterior work.

Who purchases the materials?

By agreement. We can purchase materials with receipts and transparent pricing, or you can buy them based on our specifications.

How does payment work?

Payments are typically phase-based: deposit, progress payments by completed milestones, and a final payment after acceptance.

Do you provide a workmanship warranty?

Yes, workmanship is covered by warranty. Terms are clearly defined in the contract before work begins.
