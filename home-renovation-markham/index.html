What does a general contractor do in Markham?

A general contractor in Markham manages the full scope of a construction or renovation project: coordinating trades (framing, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, finishing), obtaining permits, scheduling inspections, and ensuring the project is delivered on time, on budget, and to code.

How much does a general contractor charge in Markham?

General contractors in Markham typically charge 15–25% of total project cost as a management fee, or provide a fixed-price contract. For full renovations, our all-in pricing includes labour, materials, permits, and coordination — no hidden fees.

Do I need a permit for my renovation in Markham?

In Markham, permits are required for: structural changes, additions, basement apartments, new plumbing or electrical circuits, HVAC modifications, and most significant alterations. We manage the entire permit process and coordinate all required inspections.

How do I find a reliable contractor in Markham?

Verify: WSIB clearance certificate, liability insurance ($2M minimum), references from similar projects, written contract with detailed scope, and a payment schedule tied to milestones. Avoid contractors who ask for large upfront cash payments or won't provide written quotes.
