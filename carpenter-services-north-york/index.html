Finish Carpentry vs. Rough Carpentry in North York

Rough carpentry includes structural framing — walls, floors, roof structure, deck framing. Finish carpentry covers trim, cabinets, stairs, and anything visible in the final result. aMaximum Construction handles both, making us a one-stop shop for North York homeowners and contractors.
