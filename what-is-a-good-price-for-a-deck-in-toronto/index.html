What is a Good Price for a Deck in Toronto?

Toronto deck pricing guide for 2026

Deck Pricing in Toronto 2026

A good price for a deck in Toronto depends on size, materials, and complexity. Here's what you can expect to pay for quality deck construction in the Toronto area.

Average Deck Costs

  • Small Deck (100-200 sq ft) - $5,000-$10,000
  • Medium Deck (200-400 sq ft) - $10,000-$20,000
  • Large Deck (400+ sq ft) - $20,000-$40,000+

Price Factors

Several factors influence final deck pricing:

  • Material Choice - Pressure-treated ($15-25/sq ft), Cedar ($25-35/sq ft), Composite ($35-50/sq ft)
  • Deck Height - Elevated decks require more structural support
  • Complexity - Multi-level designs, built-in features
  • Site Access - Difficult access increases labor costs
  • Railings - Glass, metal, or wood options vary in price
  • Permits - Toronto permit fees ($200-500)

Cost per Square Foot

Expect to pay $30-60 per square foot for a quality deck including materials and labor in Toronto. Premium materials and complex designs can reach $80-100/sq ft.

What's Included in a Fair Price?

  • Quality materials meeting code requirements
  • Professional installation by licensed contractors
  • Proper structural supports and footings
  • Permit acquisition and inspections
  • Warranty on workmanship
  • Post-construction cleanup

Red Flags for Pricing

Be cautious of quotes significantly below market rates. Extremely low prices often indicate substandard materials, unlicensed contractors, or cutting corners on structural requirements.
